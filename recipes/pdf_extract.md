title: "Lemon Blueberry Bread"
originalTitle: "Lemon Blueberry Bread"
source: "2025_DinnerBook.pdf"
tags: ["breakfast"]
bg: ""
ingredients:

* 5 tbsp melted butter


* 1 cup sugar


* 1 lemon, zested and juiced


* 2 eggs


* 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour


* 1 tsp baking powder


* 1 tsp kosher salt


* 1/2 cup whole milk


* 1 cup blueberries



---

Original Source: 2025_DinnerBook.pdf

### Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Spray a loaf pan with baking/nonstick spray.


2. In a stand mixer or large bowl, combine butter, sugar, lemon zest, and juice, then mix in the eggs.


3. In a separate bowl, sift together 1 1/2 cups of flour, baking powder, and salt.


4. Add 1/3 of the dry ingredients to the wet. Once combined, mix in half the milk, followed by another 1/3 of dry ingredients, the rest of the milk, and the remaining dry ingredients.


5. Toss blueberries in the remaining 2 tbsp of flour and gently fold into the batter.


6. Bake for about one hour until golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean.


7. Cool in the pan for 20 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.


8. If desired, top with a glaze of 1/4 cup powdered sugar and lemon juice.

title: "Mike's Monkey Bread"
originalTitle: "Mike's Monkey Bread"
source: "2025_DinnerBook.pdf"
tags: ["breakfast"]
bg: ""
ingredients:

* 4 tins Pillsbury biscuits (or 2 tins Grands)


* 1/2 cup white sugar


* 1 1/2 sticks butter


* 1 cup brown sugar


* 1 tbsp + 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon



---

Original Source: 2025_DinnerBook.pdf

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a bundt pan or 9x9 dish.


2. Cut biscuits into quarters. Toss in a bag or bowl with white sugar and 1 tsp cinnamon.


3. Melt butter, brown sugar, and 1 tbsp cinnamon in a saucepan to create a smooth caramel sauce.


4. Pour sauce over biscuits.


5. Bake for 30 minutes until the caramel sets.


6. Wait at least 10 minutes before dumping onto a plate to serve.

title: "Roasted Cauliflower Soup"
originalTitle: "Roasted cauliflower soup"
source: "Weld Recipes.pdf"
tags: ["soup"]
bg: ""
ingredients:

* head cauliflower (approx. 2 lbs)


* 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil


* 1 medium red onion, chopped


* 2 cloves garlic, minced


* 4 cups vegetable broth


* 2 tbsp unsalted butter


* 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice


* 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg



---

Original Source: Weld Recipes.pdf

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss cauliflower with 2 tbsp oil and salt; bake 25–35 minutes until caramelized.


2. Sauté onion in 1 tbsp oil for 5–7 minutes. Add garlic, then broth.


3. Reserve 4 florets for garnish; add the rest to the pot and simmer for 20 minutes.


4. Let cool slightly, then blend with butter until smooth.


5. Stir in lemon juice and nutmeg; season with salt to taste.

* 3-4 chicken breasts


* 6 cups chicken broth


* 1 cup chopped green onions


* 1 small onion, chopped


* 2-3 jalapeños, chopped


* 2 Roma tomatoes, diced


* 2 cobs corn (or 1 cup frozen)


* 3 ripe avocados



---

Original Source: 2025_DinnerBook.pdf

### Instructions

1. Sauté onions and jalapeños in 1.5 tbsp olive oil until tender. Add garlic and sauté briefly.


2. Add broth, chicken, tomatoes, and cumin; bring to a boil.


3. Reduce heat and simmer covered for 15 minutes.


4. Remove chicken and shred. Roast corn in a separate skillet with 1.5 tbsp oil until browned.


5. Add shredded chicken, cilantro, and lime juice back to the pot.


6. Serve topped with roasted corn, avocado, and queso fresco.

ingredients:

* 1 lb fusilli


* 1/4 cup olive oil


* 1 small shallot, minced


* 1/2 cup tomato paste


* 2 tbsp vodka


* 1 cup heavy cream


* 1 oz Parmesan cheese


* 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil



---

Original Source: Jon and Vinny's via 2025_DinnerBook.pdf

### Instructions

1. Sauté shallot in oil until softened. Add garlic, then tomato paste; cook until brick-red (5 mins).


2. Stir in vodka and cook until evaporated. Add cream and red pepper flakes; season to taste.


3. Boil pasta until just shy of al dente; reserve 1 cup water.


4. Combine pasta and sauce with butter and enough pasta water to thin.


5. Stir in Parmesan and serve with basil.

---

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Butter ramekins or a muffin tin.


2. Place chopped onions in the bottom of each hollow.


3. Crack one egg into each.


4. Add 1 tsp cream, salt, pepper, and 1–2 tbsp cheese to each egg.


5. Bake until set (approx. 10 mins for muffin tins, 20 mins for ramekins).
