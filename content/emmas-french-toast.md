**Emma’s french toast**

4/8/2022: three eggs, brioche – brioche is too sweet and soggy. Should have left out brown sugar if using brioche

6/1/2021: three eggs, croissant bread -- got a little too caramelized

5/18/2021: using whole foods croissant bread -- so good

1/17/2021: used iggys francese bread, worked fine! Dark brown sugar was really loved. Need more butter when you flip and before each round.

Best with challah

2 eggs

½ cup milk/cream

½ cup dark brown sugar

Pinch o salt

½ tsp cinnamon

Dash vanilla

Fry in butter
