### Instructions

1. Sauté onions and jalapeños in 1.5 tbsp olive oil until tender. Add garlic and sauté briefly.


2. Add broth, chicken, tomatoes, and cumin; bring to a boil.


3. Reduce heat and simmer covered for 15 minutes.


4. Remove chicken and shred. Roast corn in a separate skillet with 1.5 tbsp oil until browned.


5. Add shredded chicken, cilantro, and lime juice back to the pot.


6. Serve topped with roasted corn, avocado, and queso fresco.
