Baked eggs 
 
Dozen eggs 
Grated parmesan or garted parmesan, asiago cheese blend 
Whipping cream 
A few shallots or five green onions, chopped 
Salt 
Pepper 
Parchment paper if using ramekins 
 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees 
Butter ramekins or a muffin tin (good for a quick breakfast for many) on bottom or edges 
Place shallots or the white part of chopped green onions in the bottom of each hollow 
Crack egg and place into each hollow 
Pour up to a teaspoon of cream into each 
Salt and pepper generously 
Add a tablespoon or two of grated cheese onto each 
Bake in the oven until set (less than 10 minutes if in muffin tins, about 20 minutes in 
ramekins, withparchment paper on top)
