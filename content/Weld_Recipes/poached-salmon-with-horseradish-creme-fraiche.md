Poached salmon with horseradish crème fraiche 
 
Ingredients: 
Yellow onion 
Salmon fillet with skin on 
Carrot 
White peppercorns 
Bay leaf 
Creme fraiche 
Horse radish (small glass jar, refrigerated) 
Lemon 
White pepper 
 
Buy a nice large salmon fillet with skin at a fis h market or good grocery store (not on a 
Monday). 
 
Mix creme fraiche with horseradish, white pepper, and a little salt (to taste). Let sit. 
 
Boil a good quantity of water (enough to cover fish) in a large pot with bay leaf, 
peppercorns, half a peeled onion, salt, and some carrots until the broth gets some 
flavor. 
 
Boil salmon with a gentle simmer in broth until cooked. (About 20 minutes) 
 
Serve with sliced lemons as garnish and horseradish creme fraiche.
