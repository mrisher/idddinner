Pork Loin Chops

Preheat oven to 250

Put in loin chops for 25-35 min, checking temp (ideal is 115)

Heat 2 Tbsp oil in cast iron till smoking, add pork chops, turn a few times, total of 1.5 min

Add in aromatics (thyme) + butter, cook until nice and brown, spooning melted butter over and over again
