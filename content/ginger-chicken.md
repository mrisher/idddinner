Ginger Chicken

oil, garlic, onions, ginger, tomatoes (small); gobi, aloo into pieces, wash, in the pan, with water, cook. salt, pepper, coriander powder, garam masala, coriander powder, turmeric, garam masala, chili powder, atta, wheat flour (durum wheat) (meera chapati atta)

when it's done, garam masala (sprinkle, half a teaspoon, sprinkled all over).

Ginger

Chicken

2 lbs chicken (breast? cut into bite size pieces)

1 tsp salt

4 tbsp soy sauce

2 tbsp vinegar

1.5 tsp black pepper

6 inch ginger, keep only the juice

0.5 tsp turmeric

marinate for 24 hours (6 hours original recipe)

cover with saran wrap

then, cooking

1.5 tsp urad dahl

1.5 tsp brown mustard seeds

1 tsp fennel seeds

1 tsp clove powder

1 tsp cinnamon powder

3 or 4 green chiles (small)

2 or 3 dried red chile

2 large onions, chopped

1 can of whole peeled tomatoes (500 g) (not liquid)

1 tbsp garlic

2 or 3 drops of red and brown cooking color

blenderize tomatoes with green chiles.

heat oil, 6 tbsp canola oil or

butter, on medium, oil heated, add red chiles, mustard, urad dahl, fennel, and stir until mustard seeds start to pop (5 minutes). Add 1 tbsp garlic, stir until the garlic is brown. add onions and saute, when brown add tomatoes (only tomatoes) add cooking color, when tomatoes are fried, add

chicken

& marinade. Fry until brown (6-8 minutes). Turn heat to low, cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring continuously. then add clove and cinnamon, mix well, and cook until totally finished.

Garnish with cilantro.
