Short Recipe:

2 lbs chicken thighs

enough yogurt to coat

enough tandoori masala to turn pink

salt

cook on stovetop on medium until cooked (easy to cook too long)

add 1/4 cup whipping cream + 1/2 tbsp butter to create gravy

conclusion:

tasted fine. Meat needed to be shred. Could have used a lot more tandoori masala

Butter Chicken \[FULL RECIPE\]

2lbs chicken breast, cut into bite size

1 cup yogurt

1 tsp salt

1 tsp chili powder

.5 tbsp crushed garlic

.5 tbsp ginger juice

2 tsp garam masala

1 tsp tandoori

chicken

masala

mix everything, marinate for 12 hours.

gravy:

150 g

butter

100 g cashew pieces

put 50 g

butter

, and 100 g cashew and put it on low heat until the cashews get pink or golden. (brown is too much)

add 500 g (whole peeled tomatoes, blenderized, not the liquid)

4 tbsp ketchup

1 tbsp - 2 tbsp (mild to really spicy) indian maggi hot chili sauce

.5 tsp tandoori masala

100 g heavy whipping cream

2 tbsp dry methi--dirty, so start with 3tbsp and soak in a bowl to rid of the dirt. fennegree (dried leaves) wash four or five times to get rid of the dirt.

stick the marinated chicken in a pot, and boil the water, cook until the water is reduced to 1/4th of the original level.

take the cashews, add the tomatoes and blenderize to a paste. (not the liquid of tomatoes). Put the 100g of

Butter in a pot, melt the

butter, add this mixture, and add ketchup, chili sauce, the tandoori masala, boiling then put on low heat, let it thicken add heavy cream and mix with the chicken, simmer for another 15 minutes, and garnish with methi.
