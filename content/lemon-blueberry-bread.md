### Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Spray a loaf pan with baking/nonstick spray.


2. In a stand mixer or large bowl, combine butter, sugar, lemon zest, and juice, then mix in the eggs.


3. In a separate bowl, sift together 1 1/2 cups of flour, baking powder, and salt.


4. Add 1/3 of the dry ingredients to the wet. Once combined, mix in half the milk, followed by another 1/3 of dry ingredients, the rest of the milk, and the remaining dry ingredients.


5. Toss blueberries in the remaining 2 tbsp of flour and gently fold into the batter.


6. Bake for about one hour until golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean.


7. Cool in the pan for 20 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.


8. If desired, top with a glaze of 1/4 cup powdered sugar and lemon juice.
