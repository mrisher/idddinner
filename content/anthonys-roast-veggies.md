Anthony’s Roast Veggies

⅓ in cubes for butternut squash

Slightly larger for other veggies

400^\\deg

Heat oil in metal owl, add herbs and cook until fragrant (1 min if dry, 2 if fresh)

Toss cubed veggies in it

Spread on baking sheet, roast for 30-40 min

(note that squash ribbons from tender neck are [edible raw](https://www.epicurious.com/recipes/food/views/pork-tenderloin-with-turmeric-squash-and-collard-greens-salad) in a salad?)
