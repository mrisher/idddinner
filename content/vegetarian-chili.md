10/17/2021; only ½ corn, replaced 5 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin with 6 tsp garam masala. It was great, didn’t taste especially indian, could have been a little spicier. Only used ½ stewed tomatoes. Left out tomato paste, too.

Dan’s vegetarian chili

1 large onion chopped

1 large green pepper chopped

1 large red pepper chopped

1 c carrots minced

½ c minced celery

4 cloves garlic

2 14.5 oz stewed tomatoes

1 8oz can tomato sauce

5 tsp chili powder (2 tsp?)

3 T worcester sauce

2 T sugar

2 tsp cumin

1 tsp onion powder

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp salt

½ tsp cayenne

½ tsp tabasco

2 cans, 15 oz each: kidney, pinto, black, corn

Get to a boil, simmer for 45 min
