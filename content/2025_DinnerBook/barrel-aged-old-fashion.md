Serves Many

1.75 L Bourbon 1/4 c Bitters 2/3 c sugar

Place all ingredients in a growler with an American oak infusion spiral. Let sit for 3 weeks. When ready, put 3 oz in a low ball glass with ice and top with sparkling water.
