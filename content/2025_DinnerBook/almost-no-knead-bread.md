15 oz white flour

1.5 tsp salt

1/4 tsp instant yeast

Mix. Then add 7 oz water

3 oz beer

1 tbsp white vinegar

Sit at room temp covered for 8 to 18 hours

10 folds, (allow knead) then pinch and roll in flour

Place on oiled parchment paper in skillet, covered with oiled plastic wrap, for 2 hours.

Preheat oven to 500 F with dutch oven inside (at least 30 minutes before needed)

Dust bread with flour Make decorative slices

Add to dutch oven, 30 minutes at 425 with lid on Remove lid for 10 minutes.
