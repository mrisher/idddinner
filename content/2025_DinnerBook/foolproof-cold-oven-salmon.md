Fresh Salmon

1 tbsp Lemon pepper seasoning

1 tbsp olive oil

2 Lemons

1.  Drizzle olive oil on top of parchment lined baking tray, place the salmon on top and sprinkle a little more oil on top of the salmon and then shake the lemon pepper seasoning, just cover salmon evenly.
2.  Slice the lemons and place on top.
3.  Place in a cold oven and then turn the heat to 400 degrees and timer to 25 min
4.  When the timer goes off after 25 minutes, itâ€™s done!
