_Epicurious_

½ cup extra-virgin olive oil

¼ cup red wine vinegar 1 tbsp Dijon mustard

1 tbsp pure maple syrup

1¼ tsp Diamond Crystal or ¾ tsp. Morton kosher salt

½ tsp freshly ground black pepper

¼ tsp garlic powder

Combine in a jar and shake. Sealed in a jar, this keeps well in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.
