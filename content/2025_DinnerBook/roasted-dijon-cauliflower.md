1 head cauliflower, cut into small florets

1 lemon

4 tsp olive oil

2 tbsp Dijon mustard fresh tarragon

salt & pepper

Preheat oven to 500Â°F. Pick tarragon leaves off stem and finely chop. Place cauliflower on sheet pan and drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and toss to coat. Roast in the oven for 14 to 16 minutes, stirring halfway through. In a small bowl, combine tarragon, Dijon, and juice of lemon. Slowly whisk in 4 tbsp of olive oil; season with salt and pepper to taste. Allow cauliflower to cool for 2 minutes, drizzle mixture and toss.
