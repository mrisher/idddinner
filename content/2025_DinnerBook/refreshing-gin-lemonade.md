Serves 2

1.5 oz gin

1.5 oz lemon juice

1.5 oz rosemary simple syrup

_For the rosemary simple syrup:_ 1 c of water and 1 c of honey. Add a couple sprigs of rosemary and steep

_For the cocktail:_ add all to a glass with ice.
