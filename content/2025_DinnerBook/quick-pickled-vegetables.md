Cucumbers, Carrots, Green Beans, Cauliflower, Asparagus

Wash 1 lb of vegetables and cut into 1-to-2 inch pieces. Place in large mason jar. Add 1 tsp peppercorns, fresh or dried herbs. Combine 1 cup white vinegar, 1 cup of water, and 1 tbsp kosher sauce in a small saucepan. Bring to simmer, stir to dissolve salt. Pour over vegetables in jars and let cool. Good for ~4 weeks.
