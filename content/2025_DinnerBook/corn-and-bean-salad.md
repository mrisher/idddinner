2 corn

1 avocado

1 can black beans 1 lime

1 tsp olive oil salt & pepper

Optional: chopped bell peppers or shallot, cayenne pepper

Grill the corn. Rinse and drain the beans. Chop the avocado. Mix in a bowl. Zest and squeeze the lime, add a splash of oil and season with salt & pepper.
