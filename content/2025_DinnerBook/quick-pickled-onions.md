1/2 c apple cider

1 tbsp sugar or maple syrup 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt

1 c water

1 red onion, thinly sliced

Whisk first four ingredients in pot and warm. Pour over onion in mason jar and let sit until room temperature, then put in fridge. Good for 2 weeks.
